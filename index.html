About Us

The Graduate Union of Taiwanese Students at UQ is a student-led association dedicated to supporting Taiwanese graduate students and promoting Taiwanese culture within the UQ community. Through networking events, cultural celebrations, academic sharing sessions, and social gatherings, we provide opportunities for students to build meaningful connections, exchange experiences, and engage with the wider university community.
